## Utilities

**[Cyber Engine Tweaks](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/107)** - Scripting framework, required by many mods

**[redscript](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/1511)** - Compiler for Cyberpunk scripts
